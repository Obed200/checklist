Isuku n'Itunganywa (Kinyarwanda)

  1. Isuku n'itunganywa ry'ahakorerwa
  2. Isuku ihoraho: Igorofa, ameza n'ahandi hakoreshwa buri munsi bigomba guhora bisukuye
  3. Itondeka ryiza: Amadosiye, inyandiko n'ibikoresho bigomba gutondekwa neza kandi bikaboneka byoroshye
  4. Ahakirirwa abakiriya (reception/front desk): Hagomba kuba hasa neza, hakira neza kandi byoroshye kumva aho ujya
  5. Imiterere y'akazi y'umwuga: Intebe, ameza n'ibikoresho bigomba gutondekwa neza kugira ngo akazi kagende neza
  6. Urumuri ruhagije: Hagomba kuba hari urumuri ruhagije rwo gukorera neza no gusoma inyandiko
  7. Ahantu heza ho gukorera: Hagomba kuba hari umwanya uhagije w'abakozi n'abashyitsi kugira ngo bagende neza
  8. Ibyapa bisobanutse: Amabwiriza n'ibimenyetso byerekeza (departments, service points) bigomba kuba bisobanutse
  9. Ibikoresho bikora neza: Mudasobwa, printer, internet na telefoni bigomba gukora neza
  10. Imitako yoroshye: Ntibigomba kuba byuzuye cyane, ahubwo bigaragaza isura y'umwuga (logo n'ibirango bya sosiyete)
  11. Umutekano n'igenzura: Kugira uburyo bwo kugenzura abinjira n'ububiko bwizewe bw'inyandiko n'amafaranga
  12. Ahantu hatuje ho gukorera: Hagomba kuba hatarimo urusaku rwinshi cyangwa ibindi birangaza
  13. Isura yakira abakiriya neza: Ahantu hagomba gutuma abakiriya bumva bakiriwe neza kandi bizeye sosiyete

Cleanliness & Organization (English)

  1. Clean and tidy: Floors, desks, and common areas should always be clean
  2. Well organized: Files, documents, and supplies should be arranged properly
  3. Reception/front desk area: Neat, welcoming, and easy for clients to understand where to go
  4. Professional setup: Chairs, desks, and equipment arranged properly for work efficiency
  5. Good lighting: Bright enough for working comfortably and reading documents
  6. Comfortable space: Enough room for staff and visitors to move easily
  7. Clear signage: Labels for departments, directions, or service points
  8. Functional equipment: Computers, printers, internet, and phones working well
  9. Simple decoration: Not too crowded, but professional (company branding, logos, etc.)
  10. Security and order: Controlled access and safe storage for documents and money
  11. Quiet working environment: Minimal noise and distractions for focus
  12. Customer-friendly appearance: Should make clients feel welcomed and confident in the company.
