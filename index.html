  1. Recruitment & staffing: manage hiring, job descriptions, and candidate selection

  4. Attendance & leave management: track time, sick leave, and approvals

  6. Training & development: identify training needs and organize sessions
  7. Employee relations: handle questions, mediate conflicts, and resolve grievances
  8. Policies & compliance: develop HR policies and ensure labor-law compliance
  9. Health, safety & security: enforce workplace safety and emergency procedures
  10. Records & documentation: maintain personnel files and HR records securely
  11. Employee engagement & welfare: run engagement initiatives and wellbeing support
  12. Disciplinary procedures: investigate incidents and apply fair corrective actions
  13. Exit & offboarding: conduct exit interviews and manage departures/admin tasks
  14. HR reporting & analytics: prepare headcount, turnover, and KPI reports
  15. Cross-department coordination: align staffing and training with other teams
  16. Confidentiality & data protection: protect employee personal data and privacy
